Injustice in Surah 10

  1. Disbelievers will have a boiling drink and a painful doom. 10:4

  2. If you enjoy life and ignore Muhammad's bullshit, Allah will torture you forever in the Fire after you die. 10:7-8

  3. Allah has destroyed entire generations. 10:13

  4. Denying the revelations of Allah is the worst sin imaginable. 10:17

  5. "Ignominy overtaketh them - They have no protector from Allah - as if their faces had been covered with a cloak of darkest night. Such are rightful owners of the Fire." 10:27

  6. On the last day Allah will kill all the disbelievers (and then he will torture them forever in hell). 10:45

  7. "His doom cometh unto you as a raid by night." 10:50

  8. The damned will feel remorse for what they have done, but it will not save them from the doom that Allah has prepared. 10:54

  9. Those who disbelieved will face a dreadful doom. 10:70

  10. Allah drowned those who disbelieved his revelations. 10:73

  11. Moses asked Allah to harden the hearts of the Egyptians so that they would not believe until they saw the painful doom. 10:88

  12. Though the Pharaoh submitted to Allah before he drowned, Allah did not accept him, but saved his body to be a sign for those who think they can slip by with a last-minute conversion. 10:91-92

  13. If you deny the revelations of Allah, you will be among the losers and will "see the painful doom." 10:95-97

  14. "It is not for any soul to believe save by the permission of Allah."
    Allah forces people to believe or disbelieve. Then rewards or tortures them for it. 10:100
