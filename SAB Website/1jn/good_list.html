1 John: Good Stuff

  1. Those who love others are "in the light"; those who hate are "in darkness". 2:9-11

  2. "We should love one another." 3:11

  3. "Let us not love in word, neither in tongue; but in deed and in truth." 3:18

  4. "Love one another." 4:7

  5. "God is love." 4:8

  6. "If we love one another, God dwelleth in us." 4:12

  7. "God is love; and he that dwelleth in love dwelleth in God, and God in him." 16

  8. "There is no fear in love; but perfect love casteth out fear." 4:18a

  9. "He that feareth is not made perfect in love." 4:18b
