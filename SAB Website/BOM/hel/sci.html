• The Lamanites (Native Americans) had "an innumerable army" that was armed with swords and cimeters. Yet no evidence for such weapons in the Pre-Columbian New World has ever been found. 1:14

  • The Nephites "began to cover the whole earth." Yet no evidence for Native Americans of Israelite descent has ever been found. 3:8

  • The Nephites built cities, temples, synagogues, sanctuaries, "and all manner of buildings." But no trace of them has ever been found. 3:9

  • The Nephites had a large timber industry. They exported "much by the way of shipping." Of course, there is no evidence for any of this now.

  • The Nephites built "many cities, both of wood and of cement." No evidence of them exists today. 3:11

  • The Nephites built ships, temples, synagogues, and sanctuaries. But no one has found any evidence of them. 3:14

  • The Nepites wrote many books and records "of every kind", but no evidence for them exists today. 3:15
