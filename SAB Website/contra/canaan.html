Did God keep his promise to Abraham?
God promised the land of Canaan to Abraham and his seed forever. Abraham and his seed never received the promised land.
Gen.13:14-17, Gen.17:8
"And the Lord said unto Abraham, after Lot was separated from him, Lift up now thine eyes and look from the place where thou art, northward and southward, and eastward and westward; for all the land which thou seest, to thee will I give it and to thy seed forever .... For I will give it unto thee .... Unto thee and to thy seed after thee."
Acts 7:5
"And he gave him [Abraham] none inheritance in it, no not so much as to set his foot on."
Heb.11:9, 13
"By faith he sojourned in the land of promise as in a strange country, dwelling in tents with Isaac and Jacob, the heirs with him of the same promise ... These all died in faith, not having received the promises."
