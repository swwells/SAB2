Giants in the bible

  1. "There were giants in the earth in those days; and also after that, when the sons of God came in unto the daughters of men...." Genesis 6:4

    This is one of the most interesting verses in the bible. Who were "the sons of God" who had sex with "the daughters of men"? Jesus and his long-lost brothers? Sex-crazed angels from hell? The bible doesn't say, but whoever they were, they seemed to have pissed off God. Because in the very next verse, he decides to kill every living thing that he had made.

  2. "And there we saw the giants ... And we were in our own sight as grasshopper, and so we were in their sight." Numbers 13:33

    These are the same giants (the Nephilium) mentioned in Genesis 6:4. Somehow they managed to survive the flood, contrary to Gen.7:21.

  3. "The Emims dwelt therein in times past, a people great, and many, and tall, as the Anakims; Which also were accounted giants...." Deuteronomy 2:10-11

  4. That also was accounted a land of giants: giants dwelt therein in old time; and the Ammonites call them Zamzummims; A people great, and many, and tall, as the Anakims; but the LORD destroyed them. Dt.2:20-21

  5. For only Og king of Bashan remained of the remnant of giants; behold his bedstead was a bedstead of iron; is it not in Rabbath of the children of Ammon? nine cubits was the length thereof, and four cubits the breadth of it. Dt.3:11

    Og's bed was nine cubits long -- large enough for a 4 meter-long giant.

  6. "And the coast of Og king of Bashan, which as of the remnant of the giants ..." Joshua 12:4, 18:16

  7. "Goliath, of Gath, whose height was six cubits and a span." (10 ft, 3 m) 1 Sam.17:4

  8. Among those killed by the "mighty man", Benaiah, was an Egyptian "five cubits high" (7.5 ft, 2.3 m). 1 Chr.11:23

  9. God destroyed the Amorites who were a race of gaints as tall as cedars and as strong as oaks. Amos 2:9
(A cubit is about 18 inches in length; a span is about 9 inches)
