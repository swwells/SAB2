Proverbs: Cruelty

  1. God will laugh at your misfortunes, mock you when you are afraid, and ignore you when you ask him for help. And if you seek him, you will not find him. 1:26-28

  2. "A rod is for the back of him that is void of understanding."
    Beat the people who don't understand with a rod. 10:13

  3. "Whoso despiseth the word shall be destroyed." 13:13

  4. Beating your children with a rod is a sure sign of parental love. 13:24

  5. God made bad people for the pleasure of punishing them. 16:4

  6. "The wrath of a king is as messengers of death." 16:14

  7. Fools are meant to be beaten. 18:6

  8. Beat your children and don't stop just because they cry. 19:18

  9. Scorners (skeptics?) should be condemned; fools should be beaten. 19:29

  10. "A wise king scattereth the wicked, and bringeth the wheel over them." A wise king will crush those that he considers "wicked." 20:26

  11. "The blueness of a wound cleanseth away evil: so do stripes the inward parts of the belly." 20:30

  12. "The wicked shall be a ransom for the righteous." 21:18

  13. Beating your children will make them less foolish. Have you beaten your child today? 22:15

  14. Beat your children hard and often. Don't worry about hurting them. You may break a few bones and cause some brain damage, but it isn't going to kill them. And even if they do die, they'll be better off. They'll thank you in heaven for beating the hell out of them. 23:13-14

  15. Whip horses and strike the backs of foolish people with rods. 26:3

  16. Beating your children will make them wise. 29:15

  17. Beat your servants (slaves), as though they were your children. 29:19

  18. If you mock your father or disobey your mother, the ravens will pick out your eyeballs and the eagles will eat them. 30:17

  19. "The wringing of the nose bringeth forth blood." 30:33
