Old Testament: Good Stuff

    Genesis (None)

    Exodus

  1. "Honor thy father and thy mother." 20:12

  2. "Thou shalt not kill." 20:13

  3. "Thou shalt not commit adultery." 20:14

  4. "Thou shalt not steal." 20:15

  5. "Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbour." 20:16

  6. Be kind to strangers, widows, and fatherless children. 22:21-22

  7. Value the truth; don't lie. 23:1

  8. Don't do what everyone else does, if what they do is wrong. 23:2

  9. Be kind to your enemies. Do good to those who hate you. 23:4-5

  10. Treat the poor fairly. 23:6

  11. Be honest. Don't kill the innocent. 23:7

  12. Don't mistreat strangers. 23:9

  13. Every seventh year the Israelites were to leave their fields unharvested, so that the poor would have something to eat. 23:11

    Leviticus

  14. "If a soul ... lie unto his neighbour ... or have found that which was lost, and lieth concerning it ... then ... he shall restore that which he took ... or the lost thing which he found ... and shall add the fifth part more thereto."
    What is stolen or wrongfully acquired should be returned to the owner. 6:2-5

  15. "Thou shalt not lie carnally with thy neighbour's wife." 18:20

  16. Leave some grapes on the vine for travelers and the poor. 19:10

  17. Don't steal or lie 19:11

  18. "Thou shalt not defraud thy neighbour, neither rob him: the wages of him that is hired." 19:13

  19. "Thou shalt not curse the deaf, nor put a stumbling block before the blind." 19:14

  20. Don't gossip. 19:16

  21. Don't hate people. 19:17

  22. "Love thy neighbor as thyself." 19:18

  23. Respect your elders. 19:32

  24. Be kind to strangers. 19:33-34

  25. Treat others fairly. Don't cheat. 19:35-36

  26. To help feed the poor and strangers, farmers should not harvest the corners of their fields. 23:22

  27. "Proclaim liberty throughout all the land unto all the inhabitants thereof." 25:10

  28. Do not oppress one another. 25:17

  29. "Honor thy father and thy mother." 5:16

  32. Don't steal. 5:19

  33. Don't lie. 5:20

  36. "Thou shalt open thine hand wide unto thy brother, to thy poor, and to thy needy." 15:11

  38. Look out for your neighbor's animals and protect them from harm. 22:1-4

  39. Be careful when making a roof to ensure that no one falls off the edge. 22:8

  41. Do not oppress the poor, whether they be slaves, neighbors, or strangers. 24:14-15

  42. "It shall be for the stranger, for the fatherless, and for the widow."
    Be kind to widows, orphans, fatherless children and strangers. Share whatever you have with them. 24:17-21

  43. "Thou shalt not muzzle the ox when he treadeth out the corn." Let the animal eat while it works. 25:4

  44. "Thou shalt have a perfect and just weight."
    Be honest when you deal with others. Don't cheat. 25:13-15

  45. Take care of those who need help. 26:12-13

    Psalms

  49. "Keep thy tongue from evil, and thy lips from speaking guile. Depart from evil, and do good; seek peace, and pursue it." 34:13-14
