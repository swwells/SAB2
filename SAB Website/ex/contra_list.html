Exodus: Contradictions

  1. How many were in Jacob's family when they came into Egypt? 1:5

  2. Is it wrong to lie? 1:18, 20:16, 23:1, 23:7

  3. Did Moses fear the king? 2:14-15

  4. Who was Moses' father-in-law? 2:18-21, 3:1, 4:18, 18:5

  5. Does God respect anyone? 2:25

  6. Who appeared to Moses in the burning bush? 3:2,4

  7. What is God's name? 3:14, 6:2-3, 34:14

  8. Can God be seen? 3:16, 4:5, 6:3, 24:9-10, 33:11, 33:20, 33:23

  9. Is it wrong to steal? 3:22, 11:2, 12:35, 20:16

  10. Was Moses a good speaker? 4:10, 4:14-16, 6:12, 6:30

  11. Who hardened the Pharaoh's heart? 4:21, 7:3, 7:13, 8:15, 8:32, 9:34, 9:12, 10:1, 10:20, 10:27, 11:10, 14:4, 14:8

  12. Did Abraham know God's name? 6:3

  13. Is incest forbidden? 6:20

  14. Did God command the Israelites to make him burnt offerings? 8:27, 10:25, 20:24, 29:16-18

  15. Did God kill all the Egyptian cattle in the sixth plague? 9:3-6, 9:19, 12:29

  16. Who owns the earth? 9:29

  17. How many gods are there? 12:12, 15:11, 18:11, 20:3, 22:20, 22:28, 23:13, 23:24, 23:32, 34:14

  18. Must Christians obey Old Testament laws? 12:14, 17, 24

  19. How many days is unleavened bread to be eaten during the Passover? 12:15

  20. How long was the Egyptian Captivity? 12:40

  21. Is God warlike or peaceful? 15:3

  22. Is it necessary to keep the Sabbath? 16:29, 20:8, 31:13-16, 34:21, 35:2

  23. Where did Moses get water from a rock? 17:1-7

  24. Can God be tempted? 17:2

  25. How did God tell Moses to get water from a rock? 17:58

  26. Who owns the earth? 19:5

  27. Who gave the law to Moses? 19:20, 20:22

  28. Is it OK to make images? 20:4, 25:18

  29. Are we punished for the sins of others? 20:5, 34:7

  30. How should parents be treated? 20:12

  31. Is it OK to call your father (or anyone else) father? 20:12

  32. To kill or not to kill. 20:13, 22:18, 23:7, 32:27-28

  33. Is it wrong to commit adultery? 20:14

  34. Is it OK to covet? 20:17

  35. Does God desire animal sacrifices? 20:24, 29:11-37

  36. Is only God holy? 22:31

  37. How should strangers be treated? 22:21, 23:9

  38. Does God approve of human sacrifice? 22:29

  39. How should we treat our enemies? 23:4

  40. Does God have a body? 33:11, 33:20, 33:23, 34:5

  41. How should Sabbath-breakers be punished? 31:14-15, 35:2

  42. Does God ever get tired? 31:17

  43. Where did Moses receive the ten commandments? 31:18, 34:4, 34:32

  44. Does God repent? 32:14

  45. Did Moses see God face to face? 33:11, 33:20-23

  46. Who wrote the (second set of) ten commandments? 34:1, 34:27

  47. Is God merciful? 34:6-7

  48. Will God destroy those that intermarry? 34:16

  49. Who made the ark of the covenant? 37:1
