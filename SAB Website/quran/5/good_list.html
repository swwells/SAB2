Good Stuff in Surah 5

  1. Don't hate other people. Treat everyone fairly. 5:8

  2. Whoever kills a human being, it is as if he had killed all mankind. Whoever saves the life of one, it is as if he had saved the life of all.
    (But see the next verse which says that the enemies of Allah and Muhammad will be killed, crucified, have their hands and feet cut off, or expelled. And after they die they will face "an awful doom.") 5:32

  3. Pay the poor-due. 5:55

  4. Feed and clothe the needy. Set a slave free. 5:89
