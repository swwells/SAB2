Genesis: Verses skeptics should know by heart


  1. In the day that thou eatest thereof thou shalt surely die. 2:17
    (God was speaking of the tree of knowledge. Adam ate from that tree and lived another 930 years.)

  2. In sorrow thou shalt bring forth children. ... Thy husband ... shall rule over thee. 3:16
    (God punished all women for whatever it was that Eve did to piss him off.)

  3. Behold, the man is become as one of us. 3:22
    (God was worried that humans had become gods.)

  4. The sons of God came in unto the daughters of men. 6:2
    (Angels had sex with women...)

  5. There were giants in the earth in those days. 6:4
    (...and had giants for offspring.)

  6. All flesh died that moved upon the earth. 7:21
    (God killed every living thing on earth -- except whatever was on Noah's ark.)

  7. Noah ... drank of the wine, and was drunken; and he was uncovered within his tent. 9:20-21
    (After the flood, Noah, the just and righteous, got drunk and naked.)

  8. Now nothing will be restrained from them, which they have imagined to do. 11:6

  9. The LORD did there confound the language of all the earth. 11:9

  10. The LORD plagued Pharaoh and his house with great plagues because of Sarai Abram's wife. 12:17

  11. I pray thee, go in unto my maid. 16:1

  12. Do to her as it pleaseth thee." 16:6

  13. He that is bought with thy money 17:13

  15. The LORD rained upon Sodom and upon Gomorrah brimstone and fire. 19:24

  16. Thus were both the daughters of Lot with child by their father. 19:36

  17. The LORD had fast closed up all the wombs of the house of Abimelech, because of Sarah Abraham's wife. 20:18

  18. And God said unto Abraham ... hearken unto her voice. 21:12

  19. Take now thine only son Isaac" ... and offer him there for a burnt offering. 22:2

  20. And Abraham stretched forth his hand, and took the knife to slay his son. 22:10

  21. Thou must come in unto me; for surely I have hired thee with my son's mandrakes. 30:16

  23. The terror of God was upon the cities that were round about them. 35:5

  24. And Er, Judah's firstborn, was wicked in the sight of the Lord; and the Lord slew him. :7
