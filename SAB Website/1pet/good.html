• "For all flesh is as grass."
    Life is ephemeral. Enjoy it while you can. 1:24

  • Avoid evil, malice, hypocrisy, and envy. Don't speak badly about others. 2:1

  • Be compassionate and courteous. Don't seek revenge when you are harmed by another. Speak kindly of others. Do good and avoid evil. 3:8-11

  • Christians should be ready to explain why they believe what they do. This is a good verse to remember when they refuse to debate you. 3:15

  • "Above all things have fervent charity among yourselves: for charity shall cover the multitude of sins." 4:8

  • Be hospitable to one another without grudging. 4:9

  • "Let none of you suffer as a murderer, or as a thief, or as an evildoer, or as a busybody in other men's matters." 4:15
