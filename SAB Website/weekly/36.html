This  week's verses: (September 1-7, 2002)

  • Jehovah's Witnesses: Dt.19:15
    Jehovah's Witnesses use this verse (along with 1 Tim.5:19) to justify their refusal to report or investigate incidents of child molestation unless two or three JWs have witnessed the event. If witnesses do not come forward (and they are discouraged from doing so since it would embarrass the organization), then the victim is told to be quiet about it -- or they may be disfellowshipped. 

  • Contradiction: Who was Joseph's father?

  • Science and History: Daniel 4:10
    Daniel's tree is tall enough to be seen from "the end of all the earth." Only on a flat earth would this be possible.

  • Absurdity: Numbers 22:28
    Balaam has a nice little chat with his ass.

  • Prophecy: Rev.3:11; 22:7, 12, 20
    "Behold, I come quickly."

  • Cruelty and Violence: Lev.20:14
    If you "lie" with your wife and your mother-in-law (now that sounds fun!), then all three of you must be burned to death.

  • Good Stuff: Luke 10:30-37
    The story of the good Samaritan gets my vote for the best bible story. We should all "go and do likewise."

  • Women, Homosexuality, and Family Values: Gen.19:8
    Lot refuses to give up his angels to the perverted mob, offering his two "virgin daughters" instead. He tells the bunch of angel rapers to "do unto them [his daughters] as is good in your eyes." This is the same man that is called "just" and "righteous" in 2 Pet.2:7-8.

  • Sex and Language: Mal.2:3
    "Behold, I will corrupt your seed, and spread dung upon your faces."

  • Intolerance: Dt.13:6-10
    If your brother, son, daughter, wife, or friend tries to get you to worship another god, "thou shalt surely kill him, thine hand shall be first upon him to put him to death." If Bible-believers followed this one, they would have to kill many of their own family and friends.

  • Injustice: 2 Th.2:11-12
    God will cause us to believe lies so that he can damn our souls to hell.
