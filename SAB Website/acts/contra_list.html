Contradictions in Acts

  1. Did Luke include everything that Jesus did? 1:1-2

  2. When did Jesus ascend into heaven? 1:1-2, 9, Acts 13:31

  3. Where did Jesus tell his disciples to go after his resurrection? 1:4

  4. Should the gospel be preached to everyone? 1:8, 8:25, 15:3, 7, 16:6, 22:21, 28:28

  5. Who were the apostles? 1:13

  6. How many believers were there at the time of the ascension? 1:15

  7. When was the Holy Ghost given? 1:16, 2:1-4

  8. How did Judas die? 1:18

  9. Who bought the potter's field? 1:18

  10. What did Judas do with the silver? 1:18

  11. Does God know everything? 1:24

  12. Does God know what is everyone's heart? 1:24

  13. Will those who call on the Lord be delivered? 2:21

  14. What must you do to be saved? 2:21, 11:13-14, 15:11, 16:30-31

  15. Did Jesus perform many signs and wonders? 2:22

  16. Who raised Jesus from the dead? 2:24, 2:32, 4:10, 13:30

  17. Was Joseph the father of Jesus? 2:30, 13:23

  18. Who buried Jesus? 13:27-29

  19. In whose name is baptism to be performed? 2:38, 8:16, 10:48, 19:5

  20. Should we obey human or divine law? 5:29

  21. Did God call Abraham before or after he moved to Haran? 7:2-3

  22. How old was Abraham when he left Haran? 7:4

  23. How old was Abraham when Ishmael was born? 7:4

  24. How long was the Egyptian Captivity? 7:6

  25. How many were in Jacob's family when they came into Egypt? 7:14

  26. Where was Jacob buried? 7:15

  27. Who bought the sepulchre in Sechem from the sons of Hamor? 7:16

  28. Was Moses a good speaker? 7:22

  29. Who appeared to Moses in the burning bush? 7:35

  30. Does God dwell in temples? 7:48

  31. Did the men with Paul hear the voice? 9:7, 22:9

  32. Were the men with Paul knocked to the ground? 9:7, 26:14

  33. Did Paul go to Jerusalem from Damascus immediately after his conversion? 9:26-28

  34. What kind of animals may we eat? 10:10-13

  35. Has there ever been a just person? 10:22, 24:15

  36. Does God respect anyone? 10:34

  37. Did Jesus come to bring peace? 10:36

  38. Are we all God's children? 13:8-10, 17:29

  39. When did Solomon's reign begin? 13:17-22

  40. Is there an unforgivable sin? 13:39

  41. Do humans have free will? 13:48

  42. Can God be tempted? 15:10

  43. Is circumcision required? 16:3

  44. Is Salvation by faith alone? 16:30-31

  45. If a husband believes, is his wife saved also? 16:31

  46. Who is the Lord of this world? 17:24

  47. Can women be church leaders? 18:26

  48. Was Jesus the first to rise from the dead? 26:23

  49. Is there to be a resurrection from the dead? 26:23
