Contradictions in 1 Timothy

  1. Does God approve of slavery? 1:9-10

  2. Can God be seen? 1:17, 6:16

  3. Should you pray for everyone? 2:1

  4. Does God want some to go to hell? 2:4

  5. Was Jesus a ransom for many or for all? 2:6

  6. Who (or what) is a ransom for the righteous? 2:6

  7. Should Christians pray in public? 2:8

  8. Can women be church leaders? 2:11-12

  9. What must you do to be saved? 2:14-15, 4:16

  10. Must everyone die? 2:14

  11. Is childbearing sinful? 2:15

  12. Is marriage a good thing? 4:1-3

  13. What should we eat? 4:1-4

  14. Should Christians be concerned with material things? 5:8

  15. Should you serve God alone? 6:1

  16. Is money good or bad? 6:10

  17. Does God dwell in darkness or in light? 6:16

  18. Should believers discuss their faith with non-believers? 6:20
