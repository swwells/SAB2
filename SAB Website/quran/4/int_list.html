Surah 4: Intolerance

  1. Those who disobey Allah and his messenger will be burnt with fire and suffer a painful doom. 4:14

  2. For the disbelievers and those who make a last-minute conversion, Allah has prepared a painful doom. 4:18

  3. For disbelievers, We prepare a shameful doom. 4:37

  4. Those who ...believe not in Allah nor the Last Day" will face a shameful doom. 4:38

  5. "Allah knoweth best (who are) your enemies." 4:45

  6. "Allah has cursed them for their disbelief, so they believe not." 4:46

  7. Christians and Jews must believe what Allah has revealed to Muhammad or Allah will disfigure their faces or turn them into apes, as he did the Sabbath-breakers. (See 2:65-66) 4:47

  8. Those who ascribe a partner to Allah (like Christians do with Jesus and the Holy Spirit) will not be forgiven. They have "invented a tremendous sin." 4:48, 4:116

  9. Allah curses those that invent lies about him, believe in idols and false deities, claim to be more rightly guided, or whatnot (like the Christians and Jews). 4:50-52

  10. Apostates are going to hell. 4:55

  11. Unbelievers will be tormented forever with fire. When their skin is burned off, a fresh skin will be provided. 4:56

  12. Oppose those that refuse to obey. 4:60-63

  13. The hypocrites refuse to die for Allah and Muhammad. 4:66

  14. Those who obey Allah and Muhammad are favored by Allah. They are the best company. 4:69

  15. Allah will bestow a vast reward on those who fight in religious wars. 4:74

  16. Believers fight for Allah; disbelievers fight for the devil. So fight the minions of the devil. 4:76

  17. "Whoso obeyeth the messenger hath obeyed Allah, and whoso turneth away: We have not sent thee as a warder over them." 4:80

  18. "So fight (O Muhammad) in the way of Allah ... Allah will restrain the might of those who disbelieve."
    So fight for Allah. Allah will always rig the battle so that Muslims will defeat non-Muslims. 4:84

  19. Allah casts the hypocrites back to disbelief. Don't try to guide those that Allah sends astray. 4:88

  20. Have no unbelieving friends. Kill the unbelievers wherever you find them. 4:89

  21. If the unbelievers do not offer you peace, kill them wherever you find them. Against such you are given clear warrant. 4:91

  22. Believers shouldn't kill believers, unless by mistake. If you kill a believer by mistake, you must set free a believing slave. 4:92

  23. Believers who kill believers will go to hell. 4:93

  24. After non-Muslims die, they will make excuses for their unbelief -- like saying that they were oppressed. Then the angels will ask them, "Why didn't you move to another country?" But the angels won't wait for an answer. They'll just take them all to hell. 4:97

  25. The disbelievers are an open enemy to you. 4:101

  26. For the disbelievers, Allah has prepared a shameful punishment. 4:102

  27. Relent not in pursuit of the enemy. They have no hope from Allah. 4:104

  28. "Plead not on behalf of (people) who deceive themselves. Lo! Allah loveth not one who is treacherous and sinful." 4:107

  29. Those who oppose the messenger and become unbelievers will go to hell. 4:115

  30. They (those who ascribe partners to Allah) invoke in his stead only females and pray to Satan. 4:117

  31. Allah will lead them astray, arouse desires in them, and they will go to hell. 4:119-121

  32. Those who believe, then disbelieve, then believe and disbelieve again will never be forgiven by Allah. 4:137

  33. For the hypocrites there will be a painful doom. 4:138

  34. Stay away from nonbelievers who mock your beliefs or Allah will treat you like them. (He'll torture you forever in hell after you die.) 4:140a

  35. Allah will gather hypocrites and disbelievers into hell. 4:140b

  36. Allah will not allow disbelievers to succeed against believers. 4:141

  37. Do not choose disbelievers as friends. 4:144

  38. The hypocrites will be in the lowest part of hell and no one will help them there. 4:145

  39. You must believe everything Allah and his messengers tell you. Those who don't are disbelievers and will face a painful doom. 4:150-151

  40. For the wrongdoing Jews, Allah has prepared a painful doom. 4:160-1

  41. Allah will guide disbelievers down the road to hell. 4:167-169
