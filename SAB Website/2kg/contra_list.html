2 Kings: Contradictions

  1. Has anyone ever ascended into heaven? 2:11

  2. Is is OK to call your father (or anyone else) father? 2:12, 6:21

  3. Did Elisha receive Elijah's mantle before or after Elijah is taken up into heaven? 2:13

  4. Were the Israelites to spare the trees in the countries they invaded? 3:19

  5. Is there to be a resurrection from the dead? 4:32-35

  6. Was Jesus the first to rise from the dead? 4:32-35, 13:21

  7. Are we punished for the sins of others? 5:27, 14:6

  8. Did God give Gehazi leprosy? 5:27, 8:4

  9. Is God the creator of evil? 6:33

  10. Is it wrong to lie? 8:10

  11. When did Ahaziah begin to reign? 8:25, 9:29

  12. How old was Ahaziah when he began to reign? 8:26

  13. Was Jehu the son or grandson of Nimshi? 9:2

  14. Did God commend or condemn Jehu for the killings at Jezreel? 10:30

  15. Where was Joash buried? 12:20-21, 13:13

  16. When did Jehoash become king of Israel? 13:1, 10

  17. Is there to be a resurrection from the dead? 13:21

  18. Does God respect anyone? 13:23

  19. How long did Jotham reign? 15:30-33

  20. Was Ahaz buried with his fathers? 16:20

  21. Should we fear God? 17:39

  22. Is it OK to make images? 18:3-4

  23. Who is the Lord of this world? 19:15

  24. Why did God turn the sundial back ten degrees? 20:7-11

  25. Is it okay to test God? 20:8-11

  26. Does God approve of human sacrifice? 21:6, 23:20

  27. How did King Josiah die? 22:20, 23:29-30

  28. Where did Josiah die? 23:29-30

  29. Who succeeded Jehoiakim as king? 24:6

  30. How old was Jehoachin when he began to reign? 24:8

  31. Did Zedekiah's eyes see the king of Babylon? 25:7

  32. On what day did the temple burn? 25:8-9

  33. How high was the chapiter? 25:17

  34. How many men were in the king's presence? 25:19

  35. On what day of the month was Jehoiachin released from prison? 25:27
