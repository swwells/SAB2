Language in 2 Nephi

  1. God will force people to eat their own flesh and drink their own blood. 6:18

  2. Filthy people will be tormented forever in a lake of fire. 9:16

  3. Wo unto the uncircumcised of heart 9:33

  4. Those who fight against Zion (Mormonism) will die, for they are the whore of all the earth. 10:16

  5. God will "smite with a scab the crown of the head of the daughters of Zion" and "will discover their secret parts." 13:16-17

  6. "Instead of sweet smell there shall be stink." 13:24

  7. "The Lord [will] shave with a razor ... the head and hair of the feet," where "hair" and "feet" are biblical euphemisms for pubic hair and male sexual organs, respectively. 17:20

  8. "That great and abominable church, the whore of all the earth." 28:18
