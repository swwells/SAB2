Contradictions in Romans

  1. Was Joseph the father of Jesus? 1:3

  2. Is Salvation by faith alone? 1:16, 2:6, 2:13, 3:20, 3:28, 4:2, 4:5-6, 4:13, 5:1, 10:9

  3. Has there ever been a just person? 1:17

  4. Can God be found through reason alone? 1:20

  5. Is it OK to call someone a fool? 1:22

  6. Should we judge others? 2:1, 14:10

  7. What must you do to be saved? 2:6, 13; 5:1; 8:6, 13, 24, 29-30; 9:11; 10:9

  8. Does God respect anyone? 2:11

  9. Is it OK to steal? 2:21

  10. Has there ever been a righteous person? 3:10, 12, 23

  11. Who (or what) is a ransom for the righteous? 3:10

  12. Should we fear God? 3:10-18

  13. Does anyone ever do anything good? 3:12

  14. Do Christians sin? 3:23

  15. What must you do to be saved? 3:28

  16. Must Christians obey Old Testament Laws? 3:28, 6:14, 7:4, 6, 10:4

  17. Was Abraham justified by faith or works? 4:2, 4:13

  18. Must everyone die? 5:12

  19. Who was to blame for original sin? 5:12

  20. Are we all God's children? 8:14, 9:8

  21. Do Christians know how to pray? 8:26

  22. Do humans have free will? 8:29-30, 9:11-22

  23. Is it possible to fall from grace? 8:38-29

  24. Does God love everyone? 9:13

  25. Does God want some to go to hell? 9:18

  26. Will those who call on the Lord be delivered? 10:13

  27. What will happen to Jews when they die? 11:26

  28. Is it OK to curse people? 12:14

  29. Should we obey human or divine law? 13:1-4

  30. How should nonbelievers be treated? 13:9

  31. What kind of animals may we eat? 14:2, 14:14, 14:21

  32. Is it necessary to keep the Sabbath? 14:5

  33. Should we try to please others? 15:2

  34. Is God warlike or peaceful? 15:33

  35. Can women be church leaders? 16:1, 16:7
