Cruelty in Surah 24

  1. Scourge adulterers and adulteresses with 100 stripes. Do not show them any pity. Have a party of believers watch the punishment. 24:2

  2. Those who spread slander will face an awful doom. 24:11

  3. Those who murmur will face an awful doom. 24:14

  4. Those who traduce virtuous, believing women ... cursed are they in the world and the Hereafter. Theirs will be an awful doom." 24:23

  5. Disbelievers will never escape the Fire that will be their home. 24:57
