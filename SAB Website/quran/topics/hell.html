Hell in the Quran

  1. And thou wilt not be asked about the owners of hell-fire. 2:119

  2. Hell will settle his account, an evil resting-place. 2:206

  3. Say (O Muhammad) unto those who disbelieve: Ye shall be overcome and gathered unto Hell, an evil resting-place. 3:12

  4. Their habitation will be hell, an ill abode. 3:197

  5. Hell is sufficient for (their) burning. 4:55

  6. Whoso slayeth a believer of set purpose, his reward is hell for ever. 4:93

  7. Their habitation will be hell, an evil journey's end; 4:97

  8. whoso opposeth the messenger after the guidance (of Allah) hath been manifested unto him, and followeth other than the believer's way, We appoint for him that unto which he himself hath turned, and expose him unto hell - a hapless journey's end! 4:115

  9. For such, their habitation will be hell, and they will find no refuge therefrom. 4:121

  10. Lo! Allah will gather hypocrites and disbelievers, all together, into hell. 4:140

  11. Except the road of hell, wherein they will abide for ever. And that is ever easy for Allah. 4:169

  12. They who disbelieve and deny Our revelations, such are rightful owners of hell. 5:10

  13. But those who disbelieve and deny Our revelations, they are owners of hell- fire. 5:86

  14. Go forth from hence, degraded, banished. As for such of them as follow thee, surely I will fill hell with all of you. 7:18

  15. Theirs will be a bed of hell, and over them coverings (of hell). 7:41

  16. Already have We urged unto hell many of the jinn and humankind, having hearts wherewith they understand not, and having eyes wherewith they see not, and having ears wherewith they hear not. 7:179

  17. When ye meet those who disbelieve in battle, turn not your backs to them. Whoso on that day turneth his back to them, unless manoeuvring for battle or intent to join a company, he truly hath incurred wrath from Allah, and his habitation will be hell, a hapless journey's end. 8:15-16

  18. Those who disbelieve will be gathered unto hell. 8:36

  19. The wicked will He place piece upon piece, and heap them all together, and consign them unto hell. Such verily are the losers. 8:37

  20. On the day when it will (all) be heated in the fire of hell, and their foreheads and their flanks and their backs will be branded therewith 9:35

  21. Lo! hell verily is all around the disbelievers. 9:49

  22. Know they not that whoso opposeth Allah and His messenger, his verily is fire of hell, to abide therein? 9:63

  23. Allah promiseth the hypocrites, both men and women, and the disbelievers fire of hell for their abode. It will suffice them. Allah curseth them, and theirs is lasting torment. 9:68

  24. Strive against the disbelievers and the hypocrites! Be harsh with them. Their ultimate abode is hell, a hapless journey's end. 9:73

  25. Those who were left behind rejoiced at sitting still behind the messenger of Allah, and were averse to striving with their wealth and their lives in Allah's way. And they said: Go not forth in the heat! Say: The fire of hell is more intense of heat, 9:81

  26. Let them be, for lo! they are unclean, and their abode is hell. 9:95

  27. Is he who founded his building upon duty to Allah and His good pleasure better; or he who founded his building on the brink of a crumbling, overhanging precipice so that it toppled with him into the fire of hell? 9:109

  28. It is not for the Prophet, and those who believe, to pray for the forgiveness of idolaters even though they may be near of kin (to them) after it hath become clear that they are people of hell-fire. 9:113

  29. Verily I shall fill hell with the jinn and mankind together. 11:119

  30. For those who answered Allah's call is bliss; and for those who answered not His call ... Such will have a woeful reckoning, and their habitation will be hell, a dire abode. 13:18

  31. Hell is before him, and he is made to drink a festering water, Which he sippeth but can hardly swallow, and death cometh unto him from every side while yet he cannot die, and before him is a harsh doom. 14:16-17

  32. (Even to) hell ? They are exposed thereto. A hapless end! 14:29

  33. And lo! for all such, hell will be the promised place. 15:43

  34. So enter the gates of hell, to dwell therein for ever. 16:29

  35. We have appointed hell a dungeon for the disbelievers. 17:8

  36. We have appointed for him hell; he will endure the heat thereof, condemned, rejected. 17:18

  37. And set not up with Allah any other god, lest thou be cast into hell, reproved, abandoned. 17:39

  38. Go, and whosoever of them followeth thee - lo! hell will be your payment, ample payment. 17:63

  39. As for him whom He sendeth astray, for them thou wilt find no protecting friends beside Him, and We shall assemble them on the Day of Resurrection on their faces, blind, dumb and deaf; their habitation will be hell; whenever it abateth, We increase the flame for them. That is their reward because they disbelieved Our revelations. 17:97-98

  40. On that day we shall present hell to the disbelievers, plain to view. 18:100

  41. Lo! We have prepared hell as a welcome for the disbelievers. 18:102

  42. That is their reward: hell, because they disbelieved, and made a jest of Our revelations and Our messengers. 18:106

  43. And, by thy Lord, verily We shall assemble them and the devils, then We shall bring them, crouching, around hell. 19:68

  44. And drive the guilty unto hell, a weary herd. 19:86

  45. Lo! whoso cometh guilty unto his Lord, verily for him is hell. There he will neither die nor live. 20:74

  46. And one of them who should say: Lo! I am a god beside Him, that one We should repay with hell. 21:29

  47. Lo! ye (idolaters) and that which ye worship beside Allah are fuel of hell. 21:98

  48. And those whose scales are light are those who lose their souls, in hell abiding. 23:103

  49. Those who will be gathered on their faces unto hell: such are worse in plight and further from the right road. 25:34

  50. Avert from us the doom of hell; lo! the doom thereof is anguish. 25:65

  51. And hell will appear plainly to the erring. 26:91

  52. Hell verily will encompass the disbelievers 29:54

  53. Is not there a home in hell for disbelievers? 29:68

  54. I will fill hell with the jinn and mankind together. 32:13

  55. But as for those who disbelieve, for them is fire of hell; it taketh not complete effect upon them so that they can die, nor is its torment lightened for them. 35:36

  56. This is hell which ye were promised (if ye followed him). Burn therein this day for that ye disbelieved. 36:63-64

  57. Assemble those who did wrong, together with their wives and what they used to worship Instead of Allah, and lead them to the path to hell; 37:22-23

  58. Then looketh he and seeth him in the depth of hell. 37:55

  59. Lo! it is a tree that springeth in the heart of hell. 37:64

  60. And afterward, lo! their return is surely unto hell. 37:68

  61. Ye cannot excite (anyone) against Him. Save him who is to burn in hell. 37:162-3

  62. Hell, where they will burn, an evil resting-place. Here is a boiling and an ice-cold draught, so let them taste it. 38:56-57

  63. I shall fill hell with thee and with such of them as follow thee, together. 38:85

  64. Will not the home of disbelievers be in hell? 39:32

  65. Is not the home of the scorners in hell? 39:60

  66. Those who disbelieve are driven unto hell in troops. 39:71

  67. Enter ye the gates of hell to dwell therein. Thus hapless is the journey's end of the scorners. 39:72

  68. Those in the Fire say unto the guards of hell: Entreat your Lord that He relieve us of a day of the torment. 40:49

  69. Those who scorn My service, they will enter hell, disgraced. 40:60

  70. Enter ye the gates of hell, to dwell therein. Evil is the habitation of the scornful. 40:76

  71. Lo! the guilty are immortal in hell's torment. It is not relaxed for them, and they despair therein. 43:74-75

  72. (And it will be said): Take him and drag him to the midst of hell, Then pour upon his head the torment of boiling water. 44:47-48

  73. He hath saved them from the doom of hell. 44:56

  74. Beyond them there is hell ... Theirs will be an awful doom. 45:10

  75. And may punish the hypocritical men and the hypocritical women, and the idolatrous men and the idolatrous women, who think an evil thought concerning Allah. For them is the evil turn of fortune, and Allah is wroth against them and hath cursed them, and hath made ready for them hell, a hapless journey's end. 48:6

  76. Do ye twain hurl to hell each rebel ingrate? 50:24

  77. On the day when We say unto hell: Art thou filled? and it saith: Can there be more to come? 50:30

  78. The day when they are thrust with a (disdainful) thrust, into the fire of hell. 52:13

  79. ...the torment of hell-fire. 52:18

  80. On the day when they are dragged into the Fire upon their faces (it is said unto them): Feel the touch of hell. 54:48

  81. This is hell which the guilty deny. They go circling round between it and fierce, boiling water. 55:43-44

  82. But if he is of the rejecters, the erring, Then the welcome will be boiling water And roasting at hell-fire. 56:92-94

  83. Those who disbelieve and deny Our revelations, they are owners of hell-fire. 57:19

  84. Hell will suffice them; they will feel the heat thereof - a hapless journey's end! 58:8

  85. O Prophet! Strive against the disbelievers and the hypocrites, and be stern with them. Hell will be their home, a hapless journey's end. 66:9

  86. For those who disbelieve in their Lord there is the doom of hell, a hapless journey's end! 67:6

  87. Take him and fetter him And then expose him to hell-fire. 69:30-31

  88. lo! it is the fire of hell Eager to roast. 70:15-16

  89. And as for those who are unjust, they are firewood for hell. 72:15

  90. Whoso disobeyeth Allah and His messenger, lo! his is fire of hell, wherein such dwell for ever. 72:23

  91. Lo! hell lurketh in ambush, A home for the rebellious. 78:21-22

  92. And hell will stand forth visible to him who seeth, Then, as for him who rebelled And chose the life of the world, Lo! hell will be his home. 79:36-39

  93. And lo! the wicked verily will be in hell; They will burn therein on the Day of Judgment. 82:14-15

  94. They verily will burn in hell. 83:16

  95. Lo! they who persecute believing men and believing women and repent not, theirs verily will be the doom of hell, and theirs the doom of burning. 85:10
