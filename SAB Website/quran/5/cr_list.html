Cruelty in Surah 5

  1. "Fear Me! 5:3

  2. Those who deny Islam will be losers in the Hereafter. 5:5

  3. Disbelievers are the rightful owners of Hell. 5:10

  4. "The owners of the fire. That is the reward of evil-doers." 5:29

  5. Those who make war with Allah and his messenger will be killed or crucified, or have their hands and feet on alternate sides cut off, or will be expelled out of the land. That is how they will be treated in this world, and in the next they will have an awful doom. 5:33

  6. Disbelievers will have a painful doom. 5:36

  7. Disbelievers will want to come out of the Fire, but will not. Their will be a lasting doom. 5:37

  8. Cut off the hands of thieves. It is an exemplary punishment from Allah. 5:38

  9. Allah makes some people sin. He will not cleanse their hearts. They will have ignominy in this world, and in the Hereafter an awful doom. 5:41

  10. Life for life, eye for eye, nose for nose, ear for ear, and tooth for tooth. 5:45

  11. Christians will be burned in the Fire. 5:72

  12. Christians are wrong about the Trinity. For that they will have a painful doom. 5:73

  13. Muslims that make friends with disbelievers will face a doom prepared for them by Allah. 5:80

  14. Disbelievers will be owners of hell-fire. 5:86

  15. Allah will test believers to see if they are afraid. Those who fail a second test will suffer a a painful doom. 5:94

  16. After agreeing to send down a table of food from heaven, Jesus warns his disciples that will catch holy hell if they ever stop believing. 5:115
