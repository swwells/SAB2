"Show us the straight path,he path of those whom Thou hast favoured; Not the (path) of those who earn Thine anger nor of those who go astray." -- 1:6-7

Surah 1 for Skeptics

There's not much to see here.

Just a prayer to Allah, praising Allah, and yet supposedly written by Allah, telling the world that Allah is awesome, Muslims are Allah's favorites and are on the right path, and everyone else has either gone astray (like the Christians) or earned Allah's wrath (like the Jews).

But you probably already knew all that.
