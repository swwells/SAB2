Prophecy Short List

  1. Behold, the LORD hath put a lying spirit in the mouth of all these thy prophets, and the LORD hath spoken evil concerning thee. -- 1 Kg.22:23

  2. Now therefore, behold, the LORD hath put a lying spirit in the mouth of these thy prophets, and the LORD hath spoken evil against thee. -- 2 Chr.18:22

  3. The prophets prophesy lies in my name. -- Jer.14:14

  4. And if the prophet be deceived when he hath spoken a thing, I the LORD have deceived that prophet, and I will stretch out my hand upon him, and will destroy him. -- Ezekiel 14:9

  5. And it shall come to pass, that when any shall yet prophesy, then his father and his mother that begat him shall say unto him, Thou shalt not live; for thou speakest lies in the name of the LORD: and his father and his mother that begat him shall thrust him through when he prophesieth. -- Zech.13:3

  6. Verily I say unto you, Ye shall not have gone over the cities of Israel, till the Son of man be come. -- Mt.10:23

  7. Verily I say unto you, There be some standing here, which shall not taste of death, till they see the Son of man coming in his kingdom. -- Mt.16:28

  8. Verily I say unto you, This generation shall not pass, till all these things be fulfilled. -- Mt.24:34

  9. Verily I say unto you, That there be some of them that stand here, which shall not taste of death, till they have seen the kingdom of God come with power. -- Mark 9:1

  10. Verily I say unto you, that this generation shall not pass, till all these things be done. -- Mark 13:30

  11. But I tell you of a truth, there be some standing here, which shall not taste of death, till they see the kingdom of God. -- Luke 9:27

  12. This generation shall not pass away, till all be fulfilled. -- Luke 21:32

  13. Jesus saith unto him, If I will that he tarry till I come, what is that to thee. -- John 21:22

  14. But this I say, brethren, the time is short: it remaineth, that both they that have wives be as though they had none. -- 1 Cor.7:29

  15. The Lord is at hand. -- Phil.4:5

  16. God ... hath in these last days spoken unto us by his Son. -- Heb.1:1-2

  17. In the end of the world hath he appeared to put away sin by the sacrifice of himself. -- Heb.9:26

  18. For yet a little while, and he that shall come will come, and will not tarry. -- Heb.10:37

  19. The coming of the Lord draweth nigh. -- James 5:8

  20. Who verily was foreordained before the foundation of the world, but was manifest in these last times for you. -- 1 Peter 1:20

  21. But the end of all things is at hand. -- 1 Peter 4:7

  22. It is the last time: and as ye have heard that antichrist shall come, even now are there many antichrists; whereby we know that it is the last time. -- 1 John 2:18

  23. The Revelation of Jesus Christ, which God gave unto him, to shew unto his servants things which must shortly come to pass. -- Rev.1:1

  24. The time is at hand. -- Rev.1:3

  25. Behold, I come quickly. -- Rev.3:11, 22:7, 22:12, 22:20
