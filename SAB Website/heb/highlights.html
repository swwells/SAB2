  • Those who disobeyed the Old Testament law were killed without mercy. It will be much worse for those who displease Jesus. 10:28-29

  • "It is a fearful thing to fall into the hands of the living God." 10:31

  • "Abraham ... offered up Isaac ... his only begotten son." (To the author of Hebrews this was a good thing.) 11:17

  • Moses "kept the passover, and the sprinkling of blood, lest he that destroyed the firstborn should touch them." 11:28
