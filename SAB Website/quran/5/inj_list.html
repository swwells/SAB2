Injustice in Surah 5

  1. "Fear Me! 5:3

  2. Those who deny Islam will be losers in the Hereafter. 5:5

  3. Disbelievers are the rightful owners of Hell. 5:10

  4. Allah has cursed the Jews and hardened their hearts. Nearly all of them are treacherous. 5:12-13

  5. Allah has stirred up enmity and hatred among Christians. 5:14

  6. The first act of religious violence occurs because Allah prefers one sacrifice over another. 5:27

  7. "The owners of the fire. That is the reward of evil-doers." 5:29

  8. Those who make war with Allah and his messenger will be killed or crucified, or have their hands and feet on alternate sides cut off, or will be expelled out of the land. That is how they will be treated in this world, and in the next they will have an awful doom. 5:33

  9. Disbelievers will have a painful doom. 5:36

  10. Disbelievers will want to come out of the Fire, but will not. Their will be a lasting doom. 5:37

  11. Cut off the hands of thieves. It is an exemplary punishment from Allah. 5:38

  12. "Allah ... punisheth whom He will, and forgiveth whom He will." 5:40

  13. Allah makes some people sin. He will not cleanse their hearts. They will have ignominy in this world, and in the Hereafter an awful doom. 5:41

  14. Life for life, eye for eye, nose for nose, ear for ear, and tooth for tooth. 5:45

  15. Non-muslims are wrong doers. 5:45

  16. "Had Allah willed He could have made you one community."
    But he decided to reveal a bunch of different, contradictory reliigions and then command each group of believers to kill each other in his name. 5:48

  17. Don't take Jews or Christians for friends. If you do, then Allah will consider you to be one of them. 5:51

  18. Jews and Christians are losers. 5:53

  19. Allah could replace nonbelievers with believers who would love him and do whatever he says. 5:54

  20. Don't choose Jews, Christians, or disbelievers as guardians. 5:57

  21. Jews and Christians are evil-livers. 5:59

  22. Allah has turned some Christians and Jews into apes and swine. 5:60

  23. Evil is the handiwork of the rabbis and priests. 5:63

  24. Allah has cast enmity and hatred among the Jews. 5:64

  25. Allah does not guide disbelievers. 5:67

  26. The Jews rejected and killed Allah's prophets, since "they were wilfully blind and deaf." 5:70-71

  27. Christians will be burned in the Fire. 5:72

  28. Christians are wrong about the Trinity. For that they will have a painful doom. 5:73

  29. Muslims that make friends with disbelievers will face a doom prepared for them by Allah. 5:80

  30. Disbelievers will be owners of hell-fire. 5:86

  31. Allah will test believers to see if they are afraid. Those who fail a second test will suffer a a painful doom. 5:94

  32. After agreeing to send down a table of food from heaven, Jesus warns his disciples that will catch holy hell if they ever stop believing. 5:115
