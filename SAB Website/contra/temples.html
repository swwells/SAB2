Does God dwell in temples?
God dwells in temples. God does not dwell in temples.
1 Kg.8:13
"I have surely built thee [God] an house to dwell in."

2 Chr.7:12, 16
"I [God] ... have chosen this place to myself for a house. That my name may be there for ever: and mine eyes and mine heart shall be there perpetually."
Acts 7:48
"The Most High dwelleth not in temples made with hands."
