Biblical Exaggerations

  1. The first men had incredibly long lifespans. Gen.5:5, 5:8, 5:11, 5:14, 5:17, 5:20, 5:23, 5:27, 5:31, 9:29

  2. "There were giants in the earth in those days." Gen.6:4

  3. The flood covered the highest mountain tops (Mount Everest?) with fifteen cubits to spare. Gen.7:20

  4. God drowns everything that breathes air. Gen.7:21-23

  5. All animals fear humans. Gen.9:2

  6. "The whole earth was of one language." Gen.11:6

  7. A tower high enough to reach heaven. Gen.11:4

  8. God gives Abraham and his descendants all of the land of Canaan "forever". Gen.13:14-15, 17:8, 28:13-14

  9. Sarah becomes pregnant in her old age. (How old was she?) Gen.21:1-2

  10. "And I will make thy seed to multiply as the stars of heaven, and will give unto thy seed all these countries; and in thy seed shall all the nations of the earth be blessed." Gen.26:4, 28:13-14

  11. Jacobs sons trick "every male" in a city into getting circumcised and then kill "all the males". Gen.34:24-26
