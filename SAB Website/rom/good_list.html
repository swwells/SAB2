Romans: Good Stuff

  1. "Thou therefore which teachest another, teachest thou not thyself?"
    Practice what you preach. 2:21

  2. Don't think too highly of yourself. 12:3

  3. "He that giveth, let him do it with simplicity; he that ruleth, with diligence; he that sheweth mercy, with cheerfulness." 12:8

  4. Let your love be genuine. Do good; avoid evil. 12:9

  5. Be kind to others. 12:10

  6. "Not slothful in business." 12:11

  7. "Rejoicing in hope; patient in tribulation." 12:12

  8. "Given to hospitality." 12:13

  9. "Bless, and curse not." 12:14

  10. Show empathy toward others. 12:15

  11. Associate with the lowly. Don't pretend to be wiser than you are. 12:16

  12. Don't return evil for evil. 12:17

  13. Try to "live peaceably with all men." 12:18

  14. "Avenge not yourselves." 12:19

  15. "If thine enemy hunger, feed him; if he thirst, give him drink." 12:20

  16. "Overcome evil with good." 12:21

  17. Note to teabaggers: Paul says you should pay your taxes and honor the government. 13:6-7

  18. Love one another: for he that loveth another hath fulfilled the law. 13:8

  19. "If there be any other commandment, it is briefly comprehended in this saying, namely, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself." 13:9-10

  20. Be honest; avoid drunkenness. 13:13

  21. "Let every man be fully persuaded in his own mind." 14:5

  22. Don't judge other people or cause them to fall. 14:13

  23. "Let us therefore follow after the things which make for peace, and things wherewith one may edify another." 14:19
