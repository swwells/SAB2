Injustice in 2 Thessalonians

  1. Jesus will take "vengeance on them that know not God" by burning them forever "in flaming fire." 1:7-9

  2. God will cause us to believe lies so that he can damn our souls to hell. 2:11-12

  3. God selects those who are to be saved (or damned) "from the beginning." It therefore makes no difference how good, kind, honest, or loving we may be; if god hasn't chosen us, we will be damned. 2:13

  4. Shun those who disagree with your interpretation of this epistle. 3:6, 14

  5. Those who will not or cannot work should starve to death. 3:10
