Contradictions in Galatians

  1. Who raised Jesus from the dead? 1:1

  2. Is it OK to curse people? 1:8-9

  3. Should we try to please others? 1:10

  4. Did Paul go to Jerusalem from Damascus immediately after his conversion? 1:16-17

  5. "But other of the apostles saw I none, save James the Lord's brother."
    Did Paul visit all of the disciples when he went to Jerusalem after his conversion? 1:18-19

  6. Should the gospel be preached to everyone? 2:2

  7. Does God respect anyone? 2:6

  8. Is Salvation by faith alone? 2:16, 3:11-12

  9. What must you do to be saved? 2:16

  10. Does righteousness come from following the Law? 2:21

  11. Is it OK to call someone a fool? 3:1

  12. Are those who obey the law cursed? 3:10

  13. Has there ever been a just person? 3:11

  14. Was Joseph the father of Jesus? 3:16

  15. Who gave the law to Moses? 3:19

  16. Must Christians obey the Old Testament's laws? 3:13, 3:24

  17. Are we all God's children? 3:26

  18. How many sons did Abraham have? 4:22

  19. Is circumcision required? 5:2

  20. Is it possible to fall from grace? 5:4

  21. How should nonbelievers be treated? 5:14

  22. Do Christians need to follow Old Testament laws? 5:18

  23. Should we bear each other's burdens? 6:2, 5
