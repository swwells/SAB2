86: The Morning Star
86:1 By the heaven and the Morning Star
86:2 - Ah, what will tell thee what the Morning Star is!
86:3 - The piercing Star!
86:4 No human soul but hath a guardian over it.
86:5 So let man consider from what he is created. (86:5-7) "He is created from a gushing fluid that issued from between the loins and ribs."
Semen, according to the Quran, is formed not in the testicles, but somewhere "between the loins and ribs."

(86:6) "He is created from a gushing fluid."
Humans are not created from semen, but from fertilized eggs.

86:6 He is created from a gushing fluid
86:7 That issued from between the loins and ribs.
86:8 Lo! He verily is Able to return him (unto life)
86:9 On the day when hidden thoughts shall be searched out.
86:10 Then will he have no might nor any helper.
86:11 By the heaven which giveth the returning rain,
86:12 And the earth which splitteth (with the growth of trees and plants)
86:13 Lo! this (Qur'an) is a conclusive word, The Quran "is a conclusive word."
86:14 It is no pleasantry.
86:15 Lo! they plot a plot (against thee, O Muhammad)
86:16 And I plot a plot (against them). Allah plots against non-Muslims.
86:17 So give a respite to the disbelievers. Deal thou gently with them for a while. "Deal gently with them (non-Muslims) for a while. (How long is "a while"?)
