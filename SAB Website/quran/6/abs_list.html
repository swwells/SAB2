Absurdity in Surah 6

  1. "See they not how many a generation We destroyed?"
    How could they (or anyone else who wasn't there) see Allah destroy previous generations? 6:6

  2. True enough. There isn't a book, prophet, or angel that would have caused skeptics to believe the foolish things in Islam or Christianity. 6:7-9

  3. Pets are people, too! 6:38

  4. Allah guided Noah, David, Elisha, and Lot on a good and righteous path. 6:84-87

  5. Allah created the jinn but he is not in partnership with them. 6:100

  6. Allah appointed jinns to be the adversaries of prophets. 6:112

  7. Jinns have led many humans astray. 6:128

  8. The jinn were disbelievers. 6:130

  9. "And of the cattle (He produceth) some for burdens, some for food. Eat of that which Allah hath bestowed upon you, and follow not the footsteps of the devil."
    The devil is a vegetarian. Kill and eat lots of animals so you won't be like him. 6:142
