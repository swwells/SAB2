SAB, 1 Samuel Introduction
  • God's 58th Killing:
    God kills Eli's sons. 4:11

  • God's 59th Killing:
    God smites the Philistines with hemorrhoids in their secret parts. 5:9

  • God asks the Philistines to make him five golden hemorrhoids. 6:4-5

  • God's 60th Killing:
    God kills 50,070 men for looking into the ark of the Lord. 6:19

  • God's 61st Killing:
    The Lord thundered with great thunder upon the Philistines. 7:10-11

  • "I will make a covenant with you, that I may thrust your eyes out." 11:2

  • The spirit of the Lord comes upon Samuel so he hacks some oxen in pieces and sends the pieces to all the coasts of Israel. 11:7

  • God's 62nd Killing:
    The Ammonite massacre (and another God-inspired body part message) 11:11

  • God's 63rd Killing:
    Jonathan's first slaughter 14:4

  • God's 64th Killing:
    God forces the Philistines to kill each other. 14:20

  • God's 65th Killing:
    God orders Saul to kill every Amalekite "man and woman, infant and suckling, ox and sheep, camel and ass." 15:3

  • God's 67th killing: In the Valley of Ellah 17:17-49

  • God repents of having made Saul king since Saul refused to carry out God's commandments (i.e., Saul refused to commit complete genocide). 15:11, 18

  • God's 66th Killing:
    To please God, Samuel hacks Agag in pieces "before the Lord." 15:32-33

  • Saul is haunted by "an evil spirit from the Lord." 16:14-16, 23, 18:10, 19:9

  • David buys a wife with 200 Philistine foreskins (twice the asking price). 18:25-27

  • God's 67th Killing:
    God said to David, Go and smite the Philistines. 23:2-5

  • God's 68th Killing:
    "The Lord smote Nabal, that he died." David then took his property and his wife, Abigail. 23:38

  • Killing those that "pisseth against the wall." 25:22, 26:34

  • David kills all the Amalekites again (see 15:7-8 where Saul kills them all the first time). 27:8-11

  • Saul has a witch bring back Samuel from the dead. 28:8-15

  • After being completely killed off by Saul (15:7-8) and then David (27:8-11), the Amalekites invade again. 30:1

  • God's 69th Killing:
    God tells David to smite the Amalekites again. 30:17

  • God's 70th Killing:
    God kills Saul, his three sons, and his men because Saul didn't kill the Amalekites (or maybe because he didn't inquire of the Lord). 31:1-4
