Job: Contradictions

  1. How many sons does God have? 1:6, 2:1, 38:4

  2. Does God know everything? 1:7, 2:2, 42:2

  3. Is the devil free to roam? 1:7, 2:2

  4. Who brought evil on Job? 2:7, 42:11

  5. Is death final? 7:7-9, 14:10, 12, 20:7

  6. What is the earth set upon? 9:6, 26:7

  7. Does God destroy both the righteous and the wicked? 9:22

  8. Can God be found through reason alone? 11:7

  9. Do evildoers prosper? 12:6

  10. Has there ever been a righteous person? 15:14

  11. Who (or what) is a ransom for the righteous? 15:14

  12. Do the wicked live long? 21:7

  13. Should we fear God? 28:28

  14. Who is the Lord of this world? 34:13

  15. Is wisdom a good thing? 37:24

  16. When were the stars made? 38:7

  17. Can God do anything? 42:1-2

  18. Can God be seen? 42:5
